HOW IS FAT BURNING ORGANIZED?

  • WARMING UP - Warming up is an activity that slowly raises body temperature, as well as muscle temperature, thus preparing the body for intense physical activity. Warming up, in addition to its physiological benefits, also has a great impact on psychological preparation for upcoming training.
  • MAIN PART OF TRAINING - Dynamic exercises for the whole body and cheerful spirit with raising the heart rate to a certain level, the body after 20 minutes of aerobic training begins to use fat as fuel, which leads to the melting of excess weight.
  • COOLING - Cooling has a similar role as heating. You need to go from intense physical activity to rest.
