Pilates as an ideal type of training?

With Pilates, you gain physical and mental stability, strength, relaxation and flexibility at the same time. You will build your body in a completely different way. Without aggressive and strenuous exercises, Pilates brings you a form that will be a role model for others. You will have a better posture and a general sense of balance in life. Cross the line and enter a new world!

SIX REASONS WHY PILATES IS IDEAL FOR YOU

  • HEALTHY BODY - If you want to tighten your muscles and fix the line, and at the same time learn to breathe and hold properly, Pilates is the right thing for you.
  • SLIM LOOK - Pilates largely avoids high impact, high power output, and heavy muscular and skeletal loading. People who stand upright in the right way can look 5 to 10 pounds thinner. This will improve your self-confidence.
  • USE OF PROPERTIES - Many types of Pilates practice the use of fun props such as big and small balls, soft rollers, magic rings, ribbons, expanders and more, which encourage balance.
  • STRETCHING AND STRENGTHENING AT THE SAME TIME - Stretching exercises are very dynamic unlike the static ones that are used in daily training.
  • REDUCING BACK PAIN - Pilates largely avoids high impact, high power output, and heavy muscular and skeletal loading.
  • FOCUS ON TRAINING AND FORGET THE PROBLEMS.
