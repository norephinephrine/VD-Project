Why our Hatha Yoga program?

  • HEALTHY BODY - Yoga brings the body to a state of flexibility, elasticity and strength. Contributes to increased vitality, and physical endurance. It has a beneficial effect on the flexibility of the spine and joints.
  • MENTAL PEACE - It is noticeable, after a very short time, the relaxation of mental tensions, the development of the ability to focus, concentration and the development of mental clarity.
  • STRESS CONTROL - By establishing harmony at all levels in all systems, especially the Central Nervous System, stress is brought under control as well as feelings of tension, anxiety and tension.
  • GOOD FEELING - A growing sense of strength, vitality of peace, serenity and harmony brings a feeling of new joy and relaxation at the same time.
