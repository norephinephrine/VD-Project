Aromatherapy Massage

Indulge in the treatment of essential oils and enjoy every moment.

Vanja Mićić - Massager

Aromatherapy is a special type of massage whose essential threads are essential oils that help to establish energy balance in a person. Aromatherapy gives the skin a special silky feeling, radiance and freshness.

Aromatherapy is part of phytotherapy (herbal therapy) and consists of a combination of aromatherapy and a special relax massage of the whole body, which has an effective effect on both your emotions and mood, So do your joints, muscles and emotions. A variety of essential oils used in aromatherapy massage, have a beneficial effect on the whole body, and especially the nervous system. The essential oils used in aromatherapy penetrate very quickly into the bloodstream, which spreads the drug particles throughout the body, bringing a beneficial effect on your body.

Aromatherapy is used to improve the psychophysical condition of the body and fitness. A holistic approach is practiced, which in fact means that we look at you in its entirety, that is, through the prism of four important aspects: physical, mental, spiritual and emotional.
