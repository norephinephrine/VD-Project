Jasna Bebić - Nutricionist

Let's meet, I'm Jasna Bebic, your nutritionist. If you live in Belgrade and the surrounding area and you need a personal diet (personalized diet), or an individual diet plan (your personal menu), I invite you to call or make an appointment online at the Activator.

How can I help you?

  • DIET AND WEIGHT LOSS - According to your weight and health, I make a plan for the screening of healthy foods and meals with a schedule of fluid and carbohydrate intake
  • PROPER NUTRITION - Together we will learn what a healthy diet is and why it is important for you.
  • EXERCISE AND TRAINING PLAN - Depending on your needs and physical fitness, we create a training plan that perfectly follows the whole story about diet and healthy eating.
  • PROFESSIONAL COUNSELING - It is not easy to decide on big changes, so my help and support you can expect it all the way to your destination.
The only way to stay healthy is to eat what you don't want, drink what you don't like, and do things you'd rather not
Mark Tven

What awaits you at the examination?

  • Assess your nutritional status.
  • Weight measurement on a Taniti apparatus.
  • Determination of BMI (body nutrition index)
  • Determination of body composition: percentage of adipose tissue. skeletal muscle tissue and water.
  • Analysis of eating habits, lifestyle and health status.
  • Blood pressure measurement, if necessary.
  • Bring blood tests and basic biochemistry (not older than 6 months), as well as the hormonal status of the thyroid gland (not older than 4 months).
  • The nutritionist will prescribe a diet, which is individual for each patient.

Advantages of nutritional examination

  • You get the right nutrition information from a professional.
  • Improving the overall condition of the organism.
  • Professional counseling.
  • Individual approach to each patient, making a special diet in accordance with habits and lifestyle.
  • Save time and money.
  • Carefully planned meals.
  • You are not alone in your endeavor, you have the support and motivation of experts.
