About Us

The activator was founded in 2008 as a friendly place of a sports family that gathered sports and recreation fans. The founders of this center did not want Activator to be just another gym in the city - they wanted to be the best in the professional support and equipment they provide. Thank you for being with us for a full twelve years!

The Activator Club exists out of love and dedication to sports and the idea of the Activator brand is to popularize and stimulate people, youth and children to play sports. Our goal is to help everyone who needs help and who we are able to help.

Our philosophy is based on humanistic and sports values and our team of professionals is always ready to welcome you, listen to you and provide support. It is a team with which you will have fun and get the desired results.

WHAT DIFFERS US IN RELATION TO OTHERS?

With first-class equipment, efficient fitness programs, a social atmosphere and instructors who are inspired by fitness and oriented to give you a sporty feeling, you will be encouraged to reach the desired physical shape.

  • Modernly equipped ambience and working atmosphere will motivate you to give your maximum at every training
  • A friendly and professional team that will meet all your requirements and help you reach your desired goal
  • Top results and great impressions of the members of the Activator training program
  • Many years of experience and references of members of the fitness club Aktivator
  • All programs are intended for exercisers of both sexes
